# Media Selection Report: Lake Gardenia Resort

## Objective
To select the highest quality visual assets that align with a "premium boutique nature retreat" aesthetic for the Lake Gardenia Resort website. The goal is to convey a cinematic, luxurious, calm, and memorable experience that feels handcrafted and avoids a standard hotel booking feel.

## Evaluation Criteria
Every asset was evaluated based on:
1. **Quality:** Sharpness, lighting, exposure, and color fidelity.
2. **Composition:** Framing, rule of thirds, leading lines, and clutter reduction.
3. **Emotional Appeal:** Does it evoke calmness, warmth, and a desire to escape?
4. **Luxury Perception:** Does it feel premium (e.g., ₹2 lakh custom website level)?
5. **Storytelling Value:** Does it help tell the story of a nature retreat?

## Overall Audit Summary
Out of over 89 images (including original `.JPG`/`.jpeg` files and recent WhatsApp batches), the vast majority have been filtered out due to poor composition, low lighting, or standard documentation-style angles that do not meet the premium cinematic threshold. 

We have isolated a core set of "Hero" and "Supplementary" images.

## Strategic Recommendations for Website Implementation

1. **Visual Hierarchy:** Use #24 (Bedroom at Golden Hour) and #20 (Walkway to Gazebo) as primary Hero banners. Their warm lighting fits the "calm, warm, memorable" aesthetic perfectly.
2. **Cropping:** Any image used must be meticulously cropped to hide utility structures (water tanks, sheds, green shade netting).
3. **Missing Elements:** There are no high-quality food, dining, or guest experience (people) shots. The website layout will rely heavily on landscapes, architecture, and mood. We will use typography and whitespace to carry the luxury feel where imagery is lacking.
